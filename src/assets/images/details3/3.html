iQOO U3 details

Buy For

Beware of

Verdict

Vivo iQOO U3 ensures a great usage option with its 6GB RAM and Octa-core processor framework. You will be able to go on for long, provided the device comes loaded with a 5000mAh battery compatible with 18W Fast Charging technology. However, if we consider the price range of the smartphone and the camera layout offered by other competitors, the dual rear cameras, is an average offering. The smartphone also flaunts restricted internal storage. 

Great RAM powered by massive battery
Display and Camera

Vivo iQOO U3 displays a 6.58-inch IPS LCD screen having a resolution of 1080 x 2408 pixels. The bezel-less display of the smartphone features a waterdrop notch framework with a 20:9 aspect ratio and a pixel density of 401PPI. Further, the device has a refresh rate of 90HZ.

There are dual-cameras on the backside of the smartphone led by a 48MP f/1.79 Primary Camera and another 2MP f/2.4 Depth Shooting Lens with a CMOS image sensor. Exposure compensation, ISO control, Continuous Shooting, HDR mode, Touch to Focus are a wide variety of rear camera features. An 8MP f/2.0 Selfie Shooter is placed on the front. 

Configuration and Battery

Vivo iQOO U3 comes packed with 6GB RAM accompanied by MediaTek Dimensity 800 MT6873V chipset. An Octa-core processor layout comprising 2GHz, Cortex A76  and Cortex A55 dual Quad-core teamed with a Mali-G57 MC4 GPU ensures quality user experience.

A 5,000mAh Li-ion type non-replaceable battery powers up the device. It is also compatible with 18W Fast Charging technology.

Storage and Connectivity

The internal memory of Vivo iQOO U3 is restricted to 128GB. The device supports multiple connectivity options like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth v5.1, A-GPS, USB Type-C, USB OTG, Mobile Hotspot alongside 5G and 4G VoLTE bands.
